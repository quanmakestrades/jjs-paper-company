The Problem

Running paper sales
through your inbox
isn't a system.

Email-based ordering creates delays, errors, and friction — for your customers, your team, and your bottom line. Here's what that's costing you every day.

  • ⏳
    Orders Stuck in Queues

    Customers are waiting hours — sometimes days — for confirmation on orders that should take seconds. Every delay is a relationship at risk.

  • 📋
    Manual Entry & Human Error

    Rekeying email orders into your system introduces mistakes — wrong SKUs, wrong quantities, wrong delivery addresses.

  • 📉
    No Visibility for Your Customers

    Buyers can't see stock levels, track orders, or reorder past purchases without picking up the phone or writing another email.

  • 🔀
    Multi-Vendor Complexity Hidden from You

    Juggling Pixelle, Neenah, Lindenmeyr, Domtar, and your independent suppliers in separate conversations? That's expensive coordination overhead.

The Solution

A complete operations upgrade
for your paper business.

We replace the back-and-forth with a clean, centralized platform that handles ordering, inventory, and customer communication — without adding to your payroll.

🛒

Self-Service Order Portal

Customers log in, browse your full catalog — sorted by grade, weight, finish, and supplier — and place orders on their own time. No phone tag, no back-and-forth.

📦

Live Inventory Sync

Real-time stock visibility pulled from your supplier relationships — Pixelle, Neenah, Midland, Lindenmeyr, Domtar, and more — consolidated into one clean interface.

🔁

Automated Reorder Workflows

When a customer's order pattern is established, the system can surface reorder suggestions, approve standard orders, and route them to the right vendor — hands-free.

📊

Order Tracking & History

Customers see their full order history, delivery status, and downloadable invoices in their dashboard. Your team spends less time answering "Where's my order?"

💬

Smart Quote Engine

Customers request custom quotes directly in the portal. The system pre-fills known pricing parameters and routes exceptions to your team for final approval.

📈

Sales & Demand Reporting

See what's selling, who's buying, and which product lines need attention — all in one dashboard designed for a paper distributor's actual workflow.

The Process

From inbox chaos to
automated order flow.

Getting your team operational on the new platform takes weeks, not months. Here's exactly how it happens.

1

Discovery & Mapping

We map your current vendor relationships, product catalog, and order workflows to build a platform shaped around your operation — not a generic template.

2

Catalog Build & Integration

Your full inventory — across all suppliers — is imported, organized, and connected to live pricing feeds where available. Your customers see exactly what you want them to see.

3

Portal Launch & Onboarding

Customers receive a portal login and a short walkthrough. Most place their first self-service order within 24 hours. No friction, no confusion.
